Pritha Sharma

Welcome to my portfolio! I am Pritha Sharma, a student of the MS in Learning Design and Technology program at USF. This semester, I enjoyed the learning experience of understanding different forms of Interactive Media. I have been a language teacher, and my projects this semester focused on language education using tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Movavi Video Editor, Adobe Captivate, and Adobe Dreamweaver.

   

I understood that technology plays an essential role by providing a voice to the learning designer to create meaningful experiences for the learner audience. Using various tools this semester, I felt that I can build such meaningful experiences.

   

Looking ahead, I am eager to continue pushing the boundaries of how technology can transform learning experiences. Whether through Augmented reality, gamified learning, or other innovative platforms, I aim to contribute to the evolving landscape of educational technology. I invite you to explore my portfolio and discover how my projects bring this vision to life.

  • IMA2:Usability Testing Report for a language learning app

  • IMA3:Video Production on using colors as cognitive tools for language teaching and learning
